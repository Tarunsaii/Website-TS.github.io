Today we pause to acknowledge the thousands of people, from every walk of life,
whom we have lost to the COVID-19 virus. This is a virus that does not discriminate-
it claims students and teachers, grandparents and grandchildren, bankers, bricklayers,
nurses and doctors, engineers, farmers, our neighbors, our friends, our family. As we
continue our fight, we are reminded of those who are no longer fighting alongside us.
Their life, their legacy, becomes part of our collective memory
and effort to build a better, safer and healthier future.
